# Kill Your Darlings: Curate Your Images

## Goal

The objective was not to generate as many images as possible, but to intentionally choose visuals that strengthen my portfolio and support my proof statement.

My portfolio prioritizes real screenshots of completed work. AI-generated assets are only used for decorative elements such as background textures and simple illustrations, while all portfolio projects are represented using authentic captures.
